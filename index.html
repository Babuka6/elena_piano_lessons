Who I Am

Hello! I'm Elena Chapavalova, a passionate piano and vocal teacher with extensive experience in music education. My goal is to open up the unique talent in each person and carefully guide them to their full musical potential with love and patience.

Education & Experience

  • Master's degree in Piano and Vocal Teaching, Tchaikovsky Moscow Conservatory
  • Taught in various music schools and institutions in Moscow
  • Worked in singing ensemble Madrigal
  • Continued as a private music teacher in the US since 2000
  • Worked as an accompanist in churches and musical theatre
  • Experienced as a piano player in elementary school
  • Currently focusing on building a private studio in the South Bay area
